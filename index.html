About

What you need to know

Tobin is undertaking his Masters of Philosophy in Applied Mathimatics and Data Science at The Univerity of Adelaide. Tobin's research focuses on the flow of information across networks in social media and network structure complexity. Tobin can often be overheard trying to convince undergraduates to get involved in data science and mathematics. Tobin was Valedictorian of the graduating class of Mathematics, Computer Science and Electrical Engineering, chosen for his outstanding academic grades and contributions to the community.
